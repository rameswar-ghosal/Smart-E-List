Features ...

1. Bad Weather Acknowledgement !

If Weather seems to be poor on your Scheduled date, This will notify you before from your Scheduled date as by sending a mail / Text Message / Notification Message on your profile.

2. Search Item's Place / Just Place here Only !

If You are in a City which is new to you then, it can search any Item's Place (e.g.- Milk Shop, Vegetable shop etc.) or JUST Place( e.g.:- Hospital, Bank etc. ) which you have scheduled in your list , No other app is required.

3. Export your Scheduled List !

Export Your List in 5 Formats [ CSV, JSON, XML(Excel), YAML, HTML ].

4. Import Se-L's Generated Scheduled List & Have a Look !

Import SEL's generated Schedule List of 4 Formats [ CSV, JSON, XML(Excel), YAML ] & Have a look, No other app is required.

5. Mail Your Scheduled List

First Export your Scheduled list in your choiced format & Mail to whom you want to send, No other app is required.
