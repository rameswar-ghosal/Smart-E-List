. . . SignUp . . .
  • Username : You have to choose the username you want.
  • First name : You have to write your First name.
  • Last name : You have to write your Last name.
  • Email : You have to provide a valid email id to get notification and a/c related informations.
  • Password : You have to keep password private for safety regarding your account.
  • City : You have to provide your city name.
  • PhoneNo. :You have to provide your PhoneNo. along with its country code.
After filling all details correctly , Your A/C will created successfully. Do You want to SignUp ?
. . . Login . . .
  • After SignUp, You have to login into your profile, At first time, when you enter your credentials to login it will take to Unique key generating page, where You have to generate Unique key which help to secure your a/c.
    • After successful generating a unique key, Now write up your credential correctly to access your profile.
  • After you can schedule your desired items or Search your desired item's place, shop according to current location.
Do You want to Login ?
. . . Profile . . .
  • In this section, you can schedule your Task / Item with your suitable date and time.
    • This section, you can also see your previously scheduled task. whereas In Expired Section, Expired List's Item only be visible for 24 hrs.
  • I Profile Section, you can perform various activities such as :- Export / Import your scheduled List, Mail to other people, you can search Item's place / Just place, Change Password / City & you see your PhoneNo.
Profile
. . . Export your List . . .
  • You can export your list in various format such as CSV, JSON, EXCEL, YAML, HTML without using any other apps.
. . . Import your List . . .
  • In this section, You can Import your list of various format such as CSV, JSON, EXCEL, YAML and read that scheduled List by just clicking on import button without using any other apps.
. . . Send Mail with / without Attachment . . .
  • In this section, You can send Mail to your friends and family by using this feature.
. . . Change your Password . . .
  • This option will be on your profile, through which you can easily change your password by providing new password and confirm password.
. . . Change your City and See your PhoneNo . . .
  • If you want to change your city and see your current PhoneNo., then go to this section.
. . . Forgot your Password ? . . .
  • If you forgot your password then you can set a new password by clicking on forget password.
  • You have to provide your registered Mail Id, then after you will get a password reset link on your registered mail Id and then you can reset your password.
. . . Search Item's Place / Just Place . . .
  • You can search Item's place ( Eg. Vegetable shops, Fruit Shops ), or Just place ( Eg. Hospitals, Bank ).
  • You have to provide your Current city name, place of that city and the place you want to search, as soon as you click on search button you will be getting searched results in few seconds.
. . . Forgot your UniqueKey ? . . .
  • If you forgot your unique key then, you have to provide your username and registered PhoneNo. to generate CombKey.
  • After that you will receive a CombKey on your mail, then entering that you will be entering to Edit your Unique Key.
